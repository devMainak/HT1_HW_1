Notable Achievements

  • Leading India to independence from British rule through nonviolent resistance.

  • Championing the rights of the untouchables (Dalits) and promoting social equality.

  • Advocatig for self-reliance and the use of khadi (hand-spun cloth) to promote economic independence.
